France is the largest country in Western Europe and has a population of over 67 million people. The capital city Paris is known as the "City of Light" and attracts around 30 million tourists annually.
French cuisine is renowned worldwide, with specialties like baguettes, croissants, and fine wines being iconic symbols of French culture.
The Eiffel Tower, completed in 1889, stands at 324 meters tall and has become the most recognizable landmark in France.
The French education system is highly regarded globally, with some of the world's oldest and most prestigious universities.
The French Revolution, which began in 1789, was a pivotal moment in European history that transformed France into a republic.
