India is a country in South Asia.
It is the second-most populous country in the world.
India has a rich cultural heritage spanning thousands of years.
The country is known for its diverse languages and traditions.
Indian cuisine is famous worldwide for its unique spices and flavors.
The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, is one of India's most iconic monuments.
India is also known for its contributions to science, mathematics, and technology.
India is home to various geographical features, from the Himalayas to tropical beaches.
The country has the world's largest film industry, producing hundreds of movies annually.
India gained its independence from British rule on August 15, 1947.
India is the world's largest democracy with a vibrant electoral system.
